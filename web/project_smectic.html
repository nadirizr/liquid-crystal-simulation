About the project

In this project we modelled a two-dimensions and three-dimensions cooling simulation of Liquid Crystals. We used Monte Carlo Simulation with Gay-Berne Potential. In this site you can find some educational information about Liquid Crystals, including their famous use in LCDs, as well as how they work, and a summary of what we saw and discovered.

In this project we investigate the Smectic phase of Liquid Crystals.



The molecules tend to point in the same direction and to align themselves in layers or planes.
